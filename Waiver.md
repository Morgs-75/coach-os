## 3. Acknowledgment of High-Risk Activities  

I acknowledge that boxing, and particularly sparring or contact training, is a **high-risk physical activity**.

Risks include, but are not limited to:

- Cuts, bruises, fractures, and musculoskeletal injuries  
- Head injury, concussion, or neurological injury  
- Loss of consciousness  
- Serious or permanent injury  
- Death  

I fully understand these risks and **choose to participate voluntarily**.

I voluntarily assume **all risks**, whether known or unknown, associated with participation in boxing training and sparring, including risks arising from:

- Physical exertion

- The actions or omissions of the Trainer or other participants

I declare that:

- I am physically and medically fit to participate in boxing training and sparring  
- I do not suffer from any medical condition that would make participation unsafe

## 6. Illness, Injury & Symptoms  

I agree **not to participate** if I am:

- Sick, injured, or unwell

If I choose to train despite symptoms, I do so **entirely at my own risk**.
